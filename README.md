# Album of the Year Ratings on Albums
<img width="2000" height="590" alt="Github - Banner" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc156e01-9927-40e8-95cf-529c459ac547" />

No need to guess or open each album individually to see your ratings when browsing albums on Album of the Year. This extension displays your album ratings directly on album covers in pages across Album of the Year.

The extension fetches ratings only from your current logged in session/cookies and doesn't transmit them to any third party source.
- No need to login again/fetch an account API key.
- Only loads ratings on demand, no running in the background.
